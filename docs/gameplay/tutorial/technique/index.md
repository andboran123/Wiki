# Techniques

Knowing *where* to go in a run is only half the battle. The other half is *how fast you do it when you get there*. This section covers the universal techniques that apply across all seed types and all phases — the habits and micro-optimizations that separate players with the same routing knowledge but different execution speeds.

Techniques are distinct from strategies. A strategy tells you which structures to visit and in which order. A technique tells you how to move, craft, loot, and fight with minimum wasted motion. You can learn a strategy in an hour; mastering the techniques that support it takes weeks of deliberate practice.

---

## Why Technique Practice Matters

Many new players assume that improving at MCSR Ranked is primarily about learning new routes. In reality, **technique gaps account for the majority of the time difference between beginner and intermediate players**, even on identical seed types.

Consider: two players both get a Village seed with a Blacksmith. Player A knows the Village route. Player B knows the route *and* uses shift-clicking, has their hotbar pre-arranged, never opens their inventory while standing still, and sprint-jumps between every destination. Player B will consistently reach the Nether 30–60 seconds faster — not because they know something Player A doesn't, but because they execute the same knowledge more efficiently.

**Practice techniques separately from routing.** Use the Dibedy practice map or creative mode to drill individual techniques in isolation before applying them under the pressure of a ranked match. Deliberate repetition of a single mechanic — such as shift-clicking through a chest — will ingrain it as muscle memory far faster than hoping to practice it incidentally during live runs.

## Quick Reference: Top 5 Techniques for New Players

If you are just starting out, focus on these five techniques before anything else:

1. **Sprint-jump everywhere.** Never walk when you can sprint-jump. This alone saves minutes per run.
2. **Arrange your hotbar before looting.** Put your sword in slot 1, pickaxe in slot 2, food in slot 9 before you ever open a chest.
3. **Shift-click loot chests.** Click while holding Shift to instantly move items to your inventory — never drag individual items.
4. **Open your inventory while moving.** You have a few seconds while approaching a destination. Use them.
5. **Never mine obsidian unless you have a diamond pickaxe.** Iron picks on obsidian waste enormous time. Get diamond tools first, or use a portal that does not require mining obsidian.
