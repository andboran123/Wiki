# Village
Village is one of the most common seed types in MCSR Ranked, especially at lower Elo ranges. The goal is to gather resources from the village structures and chests, kill the Iron Golem, and craft a Nether Portal.
## Seed Guarantees
MCSR Ranked's seed filter ensures every Village seed has the following:
- Village spawns within **7 chunks** of the player (always at positive X and Z coordinates)
- A guaranteed **Blacksmith** building
- **3 lava pools** near the village OR the Blacksmith chest contains **8+ Obsidian** for a bucket-less portal enter
- The Iron Golem always drops exactly **4 Iron**
- At least **7 Iron** total (or 4 Iron + 3 Diamonds) including the golem drop, with food in chests
## General Strategy
### 1. Locate the Village
Immediately after spawning, look around for the village. It will always be within 7 chunks (~112 blocks). Listen for village sounds and look for rooftops.
### 2. Find the Blacksmith
The Blacksmith is identifiable by its cobblestone structure with a lava pool inside. Head there first to open the chest. Key items to look for:
- Iron Ingots
- Obsidian (8+ means you can do a portal enter without finding lava)
- Food (bread, apples)
- Diamonds (bonus — can be used for a sword or pickaxe)
### 3. Kill the Iron Golem
The Iron Golem roams the village. Kill it for **4 Iron**.
### 4. Craft Your Items
Once you have enough iron, craft:
- **Iron Pickaxe** (to mine Obsidian if needed)
- **Bucket** (to collect lava for the portal)
- **Flint and Steel** (requires 1 Iron + 1 Flint — get Flint by breaking Gravel)
### 5. Build the Nether Portal
- **Option A:** Use a bucket + water to build the portal from a lava pool
- **Option B:** If the Blacksmith has 8+ Obsidian, place the portal directly (faster)
- **Option C:** Taiga villages always have 10+ Obsidian in loot
## What to Bring Into the Nether
- Food
- Weapon (iron sword recommended)
- Pickaxe
- Flint and Steel
